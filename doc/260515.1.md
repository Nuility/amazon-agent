### v1.2.0 - Ad Agent原型 (2026-05-14)
**提交**: `f17f2d5` - Add day1 ad agent workflow prototype

**新增文件**:

- `src/services/prompt_engineering_service.py` (84行) - 提示工程服务

app.mount("/ui", StaticFiles(directory=ui_dir), name="ui")
